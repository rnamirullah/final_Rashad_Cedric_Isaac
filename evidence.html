Evidence that supports the championship hangover

>

Above are two visualizations that support the concept of a championship hangover. As we can see from each visualization, teams that win the championship tend to get worse the following year, in terms of both win% and net rating.

Here we have a scatter plot that shows the change in net rating rank (which is just where the team places from 1-30 in terms of net rating over the season). If you hover over any of the data points, you can also see the year, what team it was, and a number that describes postseason success. A 0 indicates they didn't make the playoffs. 1 - Lost in first round. 2 - Lost in conference semifinals. 3 - Lost in conference finals. 4 - Lost in NBA Finals. 5 - Won the NBA Finals again.

Championship Hangover, By: Rashad Amirullah, Cedric Scheu, and Isaac Garcia
